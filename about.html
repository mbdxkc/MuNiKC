About MuNi

A first-rate experience and a safe meeting place for the community.

Our Mission

MuNi provides a good environment at a great value to enjoy the community, food, drinks, and city camaraderie in one space. We believe in thoughtful hospitality and creating experiences that bring people together.

Located in the heart of River Market on the KC Streetcar line, we're your neighborhood spot for Mexican-Thai fusion, well-crafted drinks, and everything Kansas City.
